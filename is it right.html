An Effective Monthly* Choice

When and how you start using NuvaRing will depend on your healthcare provider's recommendation and your current birth control method. In a given 4-week cycle, NuvaRing must be inserted into your vagina, removed after 3 weeks (21 days), and a new ring inserted 1 week (7 days) later. The new ring must be inserted on the same day, at the same time as the previous ring. Regularly check that NuvaRing is in your vagina.
